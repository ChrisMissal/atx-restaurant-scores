The Austin/Travis County Health and Human Services Department conducts the permitting and inspection of over 4000 food establishments in Austin, several local municipalities and rural Travis County.

This website demonstrates the ability to query the City of Austin data portal to retrieve restaurant inspection score information.

Resources

Visit the City of Austin Health and Human Services Department for more information about restaurant inspection and scoring.

Visit the City of Austin Data Portal for more information about the city's open data initiative.

About Open Austin

Open Austin is a local, volunteer advocacy group. We advocate for open government, open data, and civic technology. This service was created by our volunteer hackers, and presented as a public service.

This project is open source, and you can participate by downloading at: https://github.com/open-austin/atx-restaurant-scores.

More information about the app development can be found here: http://atxhack.wikispaces.com/Restaurant+Scores
